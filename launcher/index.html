Sentry Turret

Video Live 3D model Back to Main Page
The combination of computer and mechanical engineering

Description

Using a camera and a computer, the turret identifies and tracks any object of desire with the help of YOLO algorithm. When the sight is lined up with the target, a foam dart is launched.







Video Demo

This video explain how the idea was come up and alternate uses of this technology. Footage showing the turret in action is also included at the end.








Live 3D model

Here are the 3D blueprint of the turret in display. 3D printing and custom cut aluminum allows flexible design and convenient prototyping.




Launcher

Powerful motors launche darts out the front. With detachable magazines holding up to 40 darts, this device is a true testament of engineering.
